FAQ

You can use all the major credit cards. If you are a customer with established customer relationship with HyTest Ltd. you are able to use invoice as a payment method on our online shopping checkout process. If invoicing option is not activated for you although you are repeated customer, please contact hello@earthly.ie and notify us.

Please try again in a little while. If the payment is still not accepted, please verify your account balance. If everything is as it should, but you still can't make the payment, please contact hello@earthly.ie notify us about the problem. We can manage the order manually

Delivery charges are dependent on the shipment requirements. If the products on your order are due to special requirements (for example dry ice) extra fee will be added to the shipment charges. You can see the shipping fees on the checkout process before the payment is made.

You can easily change all your information on your account. Go to login page (https://www.earthly.ie/) and log in, then click “my account” and “edit”. Here you can change all your contact information.

When you are logged in and you add products to shopping cart, you have the ability to send offer request to us using the shopping cart. You just need to add the products and quantities you are interested in and click “offer request”. We will send you an offer. Please note that this feature should only be used for bulk quantity price requests.
